Kraunsox Quick Facts

Stevenson: From Greek Stephanos, Meaning "CROWN"

People ask us all the time how did we come up with 'Kraunsox'. It is literally our name. In Greek, Stevenson means "Crown". We dug a little deeper and noticed the fun way the pronounciation of the word 'crown'is spelled: \ˈkrau̇n\. We wanted the company to be greatly influenced by family and the essence of togetherness.
